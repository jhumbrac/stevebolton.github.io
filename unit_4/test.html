Explore Costa Rica

Pura Vida is Pure Life

Costa Rica is the ideal tropical paradise for your vacation. Tourists can enjoy the pristine beaches and see whales and dolphins play.
For Nature lovers there is a great variety of exotic wildlife and tropical plants. Butterflies and orchids, crocodiles
and sharks, turtles and Toucans are everywhere. The rainforest covering the mountains are a paradise for eco tourism.
